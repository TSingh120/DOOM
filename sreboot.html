DOOM 2016

This game was a reboot and a massive success for Doom. It was fast paced but carried the vibe of original games
despite being reinvented. It's pretty decently fact paced and atmospheric. It still is a good game to this day.
This game brought the Doom franchise back into the mainstream. I won't go into detail now because I'm genuinely running
out of time right now. But you can watch videos about the lore of the entire series here.


DOOM ETERNAL

I think this is the best DOOM game ever. One of the best games period. I love this game so much (I've never played it).
This game pulled everyone back to DOOM after DOOM 2016 brought it back to light. Its gameplay flips everything the player
knows from the previous installment on its head. It is insanely fast paced and has a huge skill ceiling. You have to learn to
manage every single resource you have. It can get VERY hard if you don't play this game right. It has 2 expansion packs after
base game, The Ancient Gods part 1 and 2. These expand on the game and the story. Here's a speedrun of the base game which
dsiplays just how good a player can get.
